About the Fellowship

The yearlong Network Fellowship brings together committed teachers to incubate solutions to the tough problems faced in public education. The program is designed to tap into teachers’ great ideas and cultivate them into real plans for change.

Our Fellows then take these concrete plans and push them into action. And that’s just the beginning. Through the grassroots organizing approach of the Teacher Action Network, each teacher’s action grows into something bigger—a movement.

Program

  • Spend a year developing and implementing a pragmatic, scalable solution (action) to an educational challenge facing your school, district, and/or union.
  • Receive a $5,000 stipend for your commitment towards achieving your action.
  • Collaborate with other solutions-focused teachers in monthly full-cohort meetings.
  • Access professional development and capacity building in project management, leadership, organizing, and social media.

Candidate Criteria

Ideal candidates are developing or established teacher leaders who embrace the grassroots organizing of fellow educators to develop and implement solutions that positively impact teaching and learning.
