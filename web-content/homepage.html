One side says our schools are failing. The other side says we’re on the right track. Either way, we all know our schools can and should get better. But any efforts at change have reached a stalemate.

When neither side will budge, nobody wins. Future Is Now believes the only ones who can break the stalemate are the teachers.

So we’ve created the Teacher Action Network. Helping teachers organize for action.
